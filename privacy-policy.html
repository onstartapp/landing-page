VIII. HOW TO OPT OUT

Location Data Processing

Settings > Privacy > Location Services. Choose the applicable App and set the “Share My Location” status to “Never”. Please refer to this instruction: https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT203033.

Marketing tracking

Either choose option "Limit Ad Tracking" on your iOS device in Settings > Privacy > Advertising or Choose option “Allow Apps to Request to Track" off your iOS device in Settings > Privacy > Tracking. Please refer to instructions here: https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202074, https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT212025

After you complete an opt-out you may still receive contextual ads that are based on non-personal information.
